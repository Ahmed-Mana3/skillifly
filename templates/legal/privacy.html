Our Commitment

At Skillifly, your privacy is fundamental to everything we do. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly and information generated through your use of the Service:

Provided by You

  • Name and email address
  • Portfolio content
  • Payment info

Collected Automatically

  • Device information
  • IP address & location
  • Usage patterns
2

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the platform
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Protect against fraud and ensure security
3

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted third-party services solely to operate the platform, such as payment processors (Kashier) for transaction handling. These partners are bound by strict data protection agreements. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure password hashing, and regular security audits. While no system is completely impenetrable, we continuously work to strengthen our defenses and promptly address any vulnerabilities.

5

Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access your data
Correct inaccuracies
Request deletion
Export your data
6

Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising. Analytics cookies, if used, are anonymized and solely help us understand how the platform is used so we can make it better.

7

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, contact us at support@skillifly.cloud or visit our Contact page.
